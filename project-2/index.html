Killer Garlic Bread

Recipe and website by: Michael Friedman

"After waking up, I take my vitamins and eat fruit or, sometimes, bread with garlic, which is good for your health."
-Jordi Molla

Total Time: 12 Minutes

Servings: About 4

Ingredients:


  • 5 Garlic Cloves
    • MUST BE FRESH. NO POWDER!
  • 4tbsp Butter
  • 3tbsp E.V.O.O.
  • 1 Loaf of Crispy Bread, (Sourdough is perfect)
  • 1/2 cup Grated Cheese (Romano or Parmesan are great!)

Optional Mandatory (to taste):

  • Fresh Basil (chopped)
  • Fresh Rosemary (chopped)

Before we even get into the directions, let me tell you a little bit about this amazing garlic bread.

This garlic bread deserves to be capitalized. Killer Garlic BreadTM is so good, that it will blow your mind.

You will crave it. Your soul will merge with it when you take the first bite.
