Killer Garlic Bread

Recipe and website by: Michael Friedman

Directions:


  1. Crush and chop garlic.
    • Pro tip: Place a knife on its side, over the garlic, and hit it with your palm. BE CAREFUL!
  2. Slice bread on one side and spread open (like a SubwayTM sandwich).
  3. Place bread in oven on Broil setting until golden brown.
  4. Combine garlic, oil, and butter in a microwave safe dish.
  5. Microwave for 1-2 minutes or until melted.
  6. Brush Pour mixture onto bread liberally.
  7. Sprinkle cheese and herbs onto bread.
  8. Place back into oven for 30 seconds to brown.
  9. Cut into pieces and serve!
  10. SPEND ALL YOUR MONEY ON GARLIC BREAD STUFF


Warning: Children under 12 should not use an oven or knives without parental supervision.
