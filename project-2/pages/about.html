About the Author:

Michael Friedman is a 28 year old computer technician who is returning to school to move up in ranks in the information technology field. He has a very deep love for people, nature, and garlic bread.

In the next 5 years, Michael hopes to open and run a garlic bread themed tech support company. Maybe he'll bake fresh garlic bread and give it away with repairs! Maybe he'll have a bakery/tech repair store!

Only one thing is for certain though: he loves his garlic bread!!!

Michael lives with his girlfriend, Marie, and his cat, named Claire. Claire was a shelter cat that Michael rescued 4 years ago.
